# SmartFood – User Guide

## Overview
SmartFood is a web-based food delivery and restaurant management system that connects customers, restaurant owners, riders, and administrators on a single platform.

This guide explains how each type of user can interact with the system.

---

## 1. Getting Started

1.  **Open the application** in a web browser.
2.  Click on **Sign Up** to create a new account.
3.  **Select your role**:
    - Customer
    - Restaurant Owner
    - Rider
4.  **Log in** using your credentials or Google OAuth.

---

## 2. Customer Guide

### Browsing Restaurants
- Go to the **Customer Dashboard**.
- View the list of available restaurants.
- Select a restaurant to explore its menu.

### Ordering Food
- Add items to your cart.
- Open the cart and proceed to checkout.
- Enter delivery details.
- Complete payment using **Stripe** (test mode).

### Tracking Orders
- After placing an order, go to the **Tracking Page**.
- View real-time rider location on the map.
- Monitor order status updates (Pending, Preparing, Out for Delivery, etc.).

### Order History
- Access previous orders via the profile or history tab.
- Reorder items easily from past purchases.

---

## 3. Restaurant Owner Guide

### Dashboard
- View current orders and daily statistics at a glance.

### Menu Management
- **Add** new menu items with descriptions and prices.
- **Edit or delete** existing items.
- **Manage categories** to organize your offerings.

### Order Management
- Accept or reject incoming orders in real-time.
- Update order status: **Confirmed** → **Preparing** → **Ready**.

### Analytics
- View revenue reports and performance insights to grow your business.

---

## 4. Rider Guide

### Delivery Requests
- Receive instant delivery notifications for nearby orders.
- Accept or decline requests based on availability.

### Delivery Process
- Update delivery status as you go: **Accepted** → **Picked Up** → **Delivered**.

### Live Location Tracking
- The system automatically updates your location for the customer during active deliveries.

### Earnings & Performance
- View completed deliveries and total earnings in your personal dashboard.

---

## 5. Admin Guide

### User Management
- View all registered users (Customers, Owners, Riders).
- **Ban or unban** accounts to maintain platform safety.

### Restaurant Management
- Approve or reject new restaurant registrations to ensure quality.

### Analytics Dashboard
- View system-wide performance metrics, including total orders and platform revenue.

---

## 6. Technical Notes

- **Authentication:** All users must log in to access their specific dashboards.
- **Payments:** Payments are processed in **Stripe Sandbox mode** (no real money is charged).
- **Compatibility:** Optimized for modern web browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).
- **Validation:** Integrated form validation ensures data integrity.
